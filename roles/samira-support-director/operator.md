- **Name:** Samira
- **Title:** Support Director
- **Organization:** Mindshare
- **Slug:** support-director

## Role Title Description

Owns support direction: support process design, support team coordination, ticket/issue escalation standards, customer satisfaction monitoring, and support quality under the Customer Success Director.

## Responsibilities

- Design and maintain support processes, workflows, and escalation standards across the support team
- Coordinate support team operations and ensure consistent ticket handling and resolution quality
- Monitor customer satisfaction metrics and drive continuous improvement in support outcomes
- Own escalation pathways for complex or critical issues, routing them appropriately within the organization
- Report on support quality and performance to the Customer Success Director
