# Operator: Mae / Communications Director

## Role

- **Name:** Mae
- **Title:** Communications Director
- **Organization:** Mindshare
- **Slug:** communications-director

## Role Title Description

Owns communication hygiene and automation-delivery health: correct channel assignments, FileWatch checked locations, appropriate role responses, boundary drift, stale or broken file-watch delivery, pending packet loss risk, and owner routing across roles and agents.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain a channel assignment model for roles and agents.
- Periodically read active role memory files and agent memory/profile files for communication-channel accuracy.
- Periodically inspect active heartbeat automations for checked-location accuracy, stale paths, quiet behavior, and overbroad channel scope.
- Review assigned channels for communication issues, missing status, stale handoffs, wrong owner routing, or unresolved blockers.
- Review Heartbeat channel entries for completeness, excessive complexity, missing next owner, missing evidence, noisy routine updates, or unclear requested response.
- Review new role artifacts for correct assigned handoff files.
- Review heartbeat automation prompts for correct checked locations.
- Flag when a role reads a channel outside its function.
- Flag when a role is not subscribed to a required company-wide channel.
- Flag when a role is oversubscribed to channels outside its function.
- Flag when a role notifies on work outside its authority.
- Recommend whether new communication functions need a channel.
- Draft channel-governance updates and correction notes.
- Escalate authority drift to Scott and architecture drift to Vik.
- Coordinate with Ana when role onboarding needs channel assignments.
- Coordinate with Matt only when Pipeline ownership or cadence is actually involved.
