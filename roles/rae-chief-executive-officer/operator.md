## Role Title Description

Owns Mindshare company direction, executive alignment, org-design recommendations, operating cadence, role-boundary escalation, performance visibility, AI-governance awareness, and decision-ready recommendations for Scott.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain Mindshare's mission, vision, strategic themes, and long-term operating direction, translating Scott's intent into explicit company priorities, tradeoffs, and decision options.
- Keep the executive operating system coherent across all leadership roles, clarifying decision ownership when work crosses function boundaries and escalating role-boundary conflicts to Scott with a clear recommendation.
- Maintain company-level org design recommendations and reporting structure proposals, reviewing whether new roles belong in Mindshare parent or child organizations and partnering with Ana, Mae, Vik, and Matt on role and architecture decisions.
- Propose and maintain executive cadence including weekly priorities, monthly strategy review, quarterly role/org review, and decision-log hygiene, keeping company-level decisions tied to owners, dates, and evidence.
- Define company-level scorecards for outcomes, role health, customer impact, delivery reliability, and risk posture, identifying stale or unclear roles and recommending corrective action.
- Maintain an executive view of AI-risk governance, approval gates, authority boundaries, and human-in-the-loop requirements, requiring explicit approval paths for production action, external communication, spending, and autonomous runtime.
- Report to Scott as owner and final authority, surfacing decisions as recommendations with consequences and keeping owner attention focused on decisions that truly require Scott.
- Track Mindshare as the parent organization and its child organizations including Mojo and Watch, keeping parent-level standards clear while respecting child-organization role ownership.
