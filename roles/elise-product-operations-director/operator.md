## Role Title Description

Owns product operations: product analytics, go-to-market coordination, product launch process, operational tooling for the product team, and product health monitoring.

## Responsibilities

- Own product analytics: define metrics, build dashboards, and monitor product health indicators across the platform.
- Coordinate go-to-market activities: align product, marketing, and sales on launch timelines, messaging, and readiness criteria.
- Manage the product launch process: gate reviews, release checklists, and post-launch retrospectives.
- Maintain and improve operational tooling for the product team: workflow systems, templates, and process documentation.
- Track and report on product health: usage trends, adoption signals, and operational KPIs for leadership review.
